Joanna Rosario love for storytelling stems from watching endless soap operas with her Lola (grandmother) while she was a young girl. Having gone through physical & mental health obstacles growing up, she knew that there was more too life.

Getting sick and tired of feeling helpless of the sistuation that she was in, she persevered and said YES to persuing her dreams in the entertainment world.

During her time in Acting school, she had discovered a Psychotherapist poster at her Message Therapist's office where she was intuitively drawn to book a sesion with .

With many sessions of talk therapy, she came out of her "spiritual closet" and connected with her Guide at the Modern Mystery School,who did her Life Activation healing.

The Life Activation turned her "light on," in realizing parts of her purpose. She started to learn other healing modalities that have helped her go through her depression and nagivate through her life.

She belives that everything that she has gone through and is going through will somehow inspire and empower another person's story - so they too can say YES to their life.
